This form is for those people who had a particularly eventful time going through the Arizona school system as a student, teacher or administrator and wish to share their experiences in detail.   (Looking for the regular entry form? It's over here.)
If you are in the right place, please follow these easy guidelines in your submission:
  1. Make sure your story has a point. Try to focus on experiences that have left a lasting impact on you and/or your friends.
  2. If you attempted to fix your school's problems in any way, make sure to say so and describe the outcome.
  3. Don't make it too long, but don't make it too short either. Typical submissions are 250-400 words.
  4. Take a look at the current biography of the month if you need some inspiration.
If you prefer, you can send your story and the other information required below
to biography@fixmyschool.net instead of using this form.
