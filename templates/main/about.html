Fixmyschool.net

 provides a platform for students, teachers and administrators in middle and high schools around Arizona to submit firsthand accounts of the problems in their schools—and how they would fix them.

Our motto,

"Because the status quo is unacceptable," refers to the fact that even Arizona's best schools (with the possible exception of certain charters) are not providing the state's children with a quality education by international standards. We greatly respect the hard work that our schools have done with the limited resources they have been provided, but our education system simply must begin producing better results if we are to compete in this century's hyper-competitive global marketplace.

Our mission

is to get Arizona's education system on the path to true excellence on an international scale. This website serves that mission by allowing those people who attend or work at a school every day to participate directly in the education reform process; they can make their ideas for improvement known to a very wide audience, get feedback and encouragement from that audience, and build momentum for real change.

Please contact us if you have any questions, comments or suggestions regarding our efforts.
